# Day 06 - Enhancing the Employee Registration Form with Professional UI Design

**Date:** 29 June 2026

## Overview

Today was the sixth day of my Web Development Training. The primary focus of today's session was to improve the visual appearance and user experience of the Employee Registration Form that I had developed earlier.

Using CSS, I transformed a basic registration form into a professional-looking company website interface. The final design closely resembles a real-world Employee Management System with a modern navigation bar, company branding, hero section, interactive buttons, animations, and a clean registration form.

This practical exercise helped me understand how frontend design contributes to creating professional and user-friendly web applications.
